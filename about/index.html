About the Author

My name is Malte Dehling. I was born in Boston, MA, raised in the Netherlands, and educated in Germany. I have recently moved back to the US where I currently live in La Jolla, California.

I graduated magna cum laude with a doctoral degree (doctor rerum naturalium) in mathematics from the University of Göttingen. For my dissertation I did research on the homotopy theory for symmetric operads and homotopy Lie algebras, a topic I became interested in during my master’s. In case you’re curious, head over to my old academic homepage where I have published a few blog posts explaining my work.

More recently I have become fascinated with machine learning, and I am currently looking for work in this field.
